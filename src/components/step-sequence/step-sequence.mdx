## Examples

To use this component, pass `StepSequenceItem` as children of `StepSequence`. Each `StepSequenceItem` represents a step in the sequence.

For each item, you must provide the `indicator` prop to display the step indicator and the `children` prop to display as the title of the step.
You can set the `status` prop to `"complete"`, `"current"`, or `"incomplete"` to indicate the status of the step.

To add visually hidden text for screen readers, use the `hiddenCompleteLabel` prop to provide a label for completed steps and/or the `hiddenCurrentLabel` prop to provide a label for the current step.
The `aria-label` prop can also be used to provide an accessible label for each step.
